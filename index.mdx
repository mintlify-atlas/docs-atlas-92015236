Ophira Web is a web-based system designed to help organizations manage their fixed assets throughout their entire lifecycle — from registration to retirement. It gives your team real-time visibility into asset status, location, and history across buildings, floors, and rooms.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Ophira Web">
    Navigate to the application URL and sign in with your credentials. Your administrator provides your initial account details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your locations and categories">
    Before registering assets, create your location hierarchy (buildings → floors → rooms) and any asset categories you need. This takes about five minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register your first asset">
    Go to **Assets** and click **New Asset**. Fill in the name, category, location, and responsible party. Ophira automatically assigns an ID and generates a QR code you can print and attach to the physical item.
  </Step>
</Steps>
